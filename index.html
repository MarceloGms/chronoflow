FAQ

Q: What is the purpose of this application?

A: To create optimized schedules quickly

Q: Who are we?

A: We are Computer Engineering students at the University of Coimbra.

Q: What are the filters for?

A: Filters are used to specify certain preferences in some or all parts of the schedule.

Q: How do I generate schedules?

A: Once you have selected the relevant filters, simply click on the generate schedule button and a schedule will be displayed.

Q: How many schedules can be generated for the same person?

A: All possible variations can be displayed. By clicking on the generate schedule button, a new schedule will be generated.
